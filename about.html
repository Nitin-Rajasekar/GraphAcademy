Graph Academy



What does it do ?

The aim of the website is to help people quickly get intuitive understanding of certain algorithms


What's the idea ?


1) Provide a brief idea of what the algorithm does.

2) If necessary, explain some prerequisite terms.

3) Briefly explain the working of the algorithm

4) To solidify concepts and gain confidence, visual based illustrative questions are given at then end, which the user may solve and ascertain whether their responses were correct.
